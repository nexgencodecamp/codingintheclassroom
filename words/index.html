The Problem

The problem is as follows: Each letter of the alphabet is assigned a value (scrabble value * 5). The challenge is to come up with as many words as possible that add up to 100 points. Points are as follows:

5 point: A, E, I, O, L, N, R, S, T, U; 10 points: D, G; 15 points: B, C, M, P; 20 points: F, H, V, W, Y; 25 points: K; 40 points: J, X; 50 points: Q, Z
